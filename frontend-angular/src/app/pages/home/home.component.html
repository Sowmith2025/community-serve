FAQ

Do I need to be part of NSS?

No. While many drives are NSS-led, anyone can participate where allowed by organizers.

How are hours calculated?

Hours are based on your check-in and check-out times, validated by organizers.

Can I edit my profile?

Yes—update your name, department, and phone from your profile page.
